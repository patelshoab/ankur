Ankur Incubation Centre is just like, Incubators for babies. Same way new business is like a baby. Ankur Incubation centre nurture then and get them married, as in providing them every single thing thing for thier Start-up, Launching and Growth.

Objective of Ankur Incubation Centre is to make an ecosystem for students, alumini and faculty by providing them opportunities to transfer their business idea into reality.




Services provided by Ankur Incubation Centre are Internet Access, Infrastructure, Conference Room equipped with Multimedia, Identification of various funding option, Access to digital library and also use physical library, Machineries and Technology.

Mentoring and Guidelines of Ankur Incubation Centre are Training on Basic Entrepreneurship, Advice service on business plan formation, Marketing Strategies, Project Selections etc. Expertise guidance and Networking Opportunities in Industries
